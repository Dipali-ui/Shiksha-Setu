Frequently Asked Questions

You can enroll your child by contacting our local coordinators in Nabha or by filling out the enrollment form on our website. We'll guide you through the simple process and provide all necessary information about the program.

Our platform is designed to work on various devices including smartphones, tablets, and computers. We've optimized our content to work even on low-end devices with limited internet connectivity to ensure maximum accessibility.

We welcome volunteers who are passionate about education. You can apply through our volunteer program form on the website or contact our volunteer coordinator. We have opportunities in teaching, content development, community engagement, and technical support.

Shiksha Setu is completely free for all students and schools in rural areas. We believe in providing equal educational opportunities to everyone, regardless of their financial background. Our funding comes from government grants, corporate partnerships, and individual donations.
