About Shiksha Setu

Transforming Rural Education in Nabha

Our Mission

Shiksha Setu is a digital learning platform designed to empower rural school students of Nabha by providing them with quality and accessible education. The project aims to bridge the gap between urban and rural education systems by introducing modern, interactive, and technology-driven learning solutions.

In rural areas like Nabha, students often face challenges due to the lack of proper digital resources, smart educational tools, and modern teaching methodologies. Shiksha Setu provides an innovative solution where students can access study materials, interactive lessons, and e-learning resources through a user-friendly platform.

Learn More Watch Video

Our Vision

We envision a future where every child in rural India has access to quality education through digital means, breaking the barriers of geography and socioeconomic status. Our vision is to create an ecosystem where technology empowers teachers and students alike.

By 2025, we aim to expand our platform to cover 100+ villages in Punjab, reaching over 10,000 students and training 500+ teachers in digital pedagogy. We believe that education is the most powerful tool for social transformation.

Our Approach

Our approach is centered on collaboration, innovation, and sustainability. We work closely with local communities, government schools, and education experts to develop solutions that are contextually relevant and scalable.

We combine technology with traditional teaching methods to create a blended learning environment that caters to diverse learning needs. Our content is available in multiple languages and formats to ensure maximum accessibility.

Why We Chose Digital Learning for Rural Schools in Nabha

1. Educational Gap
Nabha is a semi-rural area where digital resources and smart education tools are still lacking. Rural students do not have proper resources for quality education.
2. Digital Divide
There is a clear difference in education quality between urban and rural areas. Limited digital infrastructure in Nabha makes this project impactful.
3. Accessibility
Nabha schools mostly use traditional teaching methods. Our project provides modern, interactive, and easily accessible education.
4. Pilot Area Selection
Nabha is a manageable and focused area where the project can be started and later expanded to other rural regions.
5. Government & Community Need
Considering local school needs, introducing digital learning in Nabha will be practical and effective for community development.
6. Scalable Model
Success in Nabha can create a replicable model for other rural areas in Punjab and beyond.
