About Shiksha Setu

Empowering rural education through digital innovation and community partnership

Our Story

Shiksha Setu was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to bridge the educational divide between urban and rural India. Founded in 2020, our journey began in the heart of Nabha, Punjab, where we recognized the untapped potential of thousands of young minds limited by geographical and resource constraints.

What started as a small initiative to provide digital learning resources to five village schools has now grown into a comprehensive platform reaching over 1,500 students across 15 villages. Our approach combines technology with traditional teaching methods, creating a blended learning environment that respects local culture while embracing global educational standards.

Our Mission

To provide quality digital education to every child in rural India, breaking barriers of geography and socioeconomic status through innovative technology and community-driven solutions.

Our Journey

2020

Foundation

Shiksha Setu was established with a pilot program in 5 village schools in Nabha, reaching 200 students.

2021

Digital Expansion

Launched our first digital learning platform and expanded to 10 additional villages across Punjab.

2022

Government Partnership

Partnered with Punjab Education Department to integrate our platform into government school curriculum.

2023

Mobile Learning

Introduced mobile learning solutions for remote areas with limited internet connectivity.

2024

National Recognition

Received National Education Award for innovation in rural education and expanded to 50+ schools.
